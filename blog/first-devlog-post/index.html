First GS2 Devlog post

Me and Jax are working on the music together!

There's songs lead by me, and then songs lead by Jax. Songs lead by me I start and try my best to make, as I'm still a beginner composer, where Jax then helps clean it up and make it better. Songs lead by Jax are ones that I give him a basic melody and idea for and he makes them. The first song (A Jax lead one) is very exciting and upbeat, and it's a remake/remix of a song from the first game! so I'm excited for you to hear it (and I'll probably use it for a GS2 trailer one day since the song is so good) The second song (lead by me) is a more literal remix of a song from the first game, which is now lo-fi and chill. I hope all of you are excited to hear the soundtrack, and remember to keep on store-storing your store storers!
