ALL ABOARD!

Hey Grocery Store Fans!
It's been a HOT minute since any devlog happened.. but I wanted to say that Grocery Store 2 is NOT cancelled !!! We've just been working very very hard on the next update.
Speaking of the next update, it's gotten bigger than expected.
We're going to be trying to add more to endings to make Grocery Store 2 WAY more fun when it comes to completing the main game. We also want to polish and up the quality. Roleplay Mode and Arcade Mode WILL come out!!
Speaking of modes, we've been working on so much more music for Grocery Store to truly hype up the experience. We're trying to work on new music for the minigames featured in games + NEW minigames that will be added.
And this is just for Grocery Store 2... don't forget, After Hours and Tower Defense are on the way! And could there be anything left for Restocked? Who knows....?
The Grocery Store Team is trying their best, so stay on the lookout for any new news or information regarding the next update(s) and games!!
