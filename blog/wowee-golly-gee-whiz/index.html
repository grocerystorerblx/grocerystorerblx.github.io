Wowee Golly Gee Whiz! Datastores?

We all know that the first Grocery Store had.. no datastores, which meant that things weren't really resettable, you couldn't restart, everything was basically hard-locked in! This time, we have actual datastores!! You can restart your progress as many times as you want! Something also coming in the game? Collectibles! I've always wanted collectibles in a game, I'm super excited for you guys to see the items I want you guys to collect! I don't wanna give too much info away now, but I will say there are 8 collectible grocery bags around the game.. so go get 'em!
